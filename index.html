FAQ

Common questions

Does sports massage have to be painful to work?

Not necessarily. Some areas can feel tender, but the treatment does not need to be unbearable to be effective. The aim is to work at a level that is productive and manageable.

Who is sports massage for?

Sports massage is not just for athletes. It can help gym-goers, runners, active people, and anyone dealing with muscular tightness, soreness, or recovery issues.

How do I book a session?
